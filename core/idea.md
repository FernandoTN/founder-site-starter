# The idea

> The raw input. One line. Everything else in this repo is derived from it.

**A personal site that's my professional home on the web — with a private back
office only I can open.**

- **Public:** who I am, what I'm building, and a way to **book time with me**.
- **Private (one sign-in):** a **CRM / Rolodex** of the people I meet, and an
  inbox of the bookings that came in from the public page.

---

In the demo this is typed live in ~15 seconds, then a team of agents turns it into
research, an identity, copy, a public site, a booking flow, and a passcode-locked
admin — deployed to a real URL, backed by a real database. Wiring it to my actual
Google Calendar is the **next** item on the ledger (`T-0007`) — and a deliberate
lesson in what *not* to connect live on stage.
