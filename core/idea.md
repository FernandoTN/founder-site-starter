# The idea

> The raw input. One line. Everything else in this repo is derived from it.

**Sprout** — the plant-care sidekick that texts you before your plants die.

---

*That's it. That's the whole starting point.* In the demo, this is typed live in
~10 seconds, then a team of agents turns it into research, a brand, copy, a page,
and a live waitlist — all traceable back to this file.

*(Optional texture, if asked: it's for busy people who love the *idea* of plants
but keep over- or under-watering them. The first thing we ship is a waitlist to
see if anyone wants it.)*
