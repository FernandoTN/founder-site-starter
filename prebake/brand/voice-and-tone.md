# Voice & tone — Fernando (personal site)

**Status:** draft canon

## Personality (three words)

**Clear · Warm · Conviction.** A builder with a point of view, talking like a
person — not a press release.

## Do

- First person, plain language, short sentences.
- Lead with a belief; back it with what you've actually built.
- Be generous and reachable. The site is a handshake, not a pitch.

## Don't

- No buzzwords ("visionary," "synergies," "thought leader").
- No unearned superlatives or unverifiable claims (see `proof-points.md`).
- No wall of text. One screen, one idea.

## Sample lines

- Hero: *"I'm building memory that belongs to you — not the platform."*
- About open: *"I'm a founder and engineer building Memori…"*
- CTA: *"Book time with me."*
- Booking confirmation: *"You're booked. It just landed in my inbox — I'll be in touch."*

## Read-aloud test

If a line sounds like a bio someone else wrote about you, rewrite it until it
sounds like *you* said it.
