# Voice & tone — Sprout

**Status:** draft canon

## Personality (three words)

**Calm · Warm · Capable.** A friend who happens to know plants — never a nagging
app, never a horticulture lecture.

## Do

- Talk like a text from a thoughtful friend: short, kind, specific.
- Lead with the benefit ("your plants thrive"), not the mechanism.
- Use one plant emoji, sparingly (🌿). Earn it.
- Be honest about being early — a waitlist, not a finished product.

## Don't

- Don't shame the user. They already feel guilty; that's why they're here.
- Don't use jargon ("photosynthetic optimization"). Ever.
- Don't over-promise. No "never kill a plant again" absolutes.
- Don't pile on adjectives. One concrete claim beats three vague ones.

## Sample lines

- Hero: *"Keep your plants alive without thinking about it."*
- Subhead: *"Sprout texts you the day before each plant needs care. One tap, done."*
- CTA: *"Join the waitlist"*
- Confirmation: *"You're on the list. We'll text you when Sprout is ready 🌿"*

## Read-aloud test

If a line doesn't sound like a calm friend would actually text it, rewrite it.
